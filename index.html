What is WenLambo?

WenLambo is a token led by Sebastc, Bs and Aslouto, it's objective is to make a sustainable ecosystem with nfts and the main token "Lambo", at the moment the project is on two chains, SmartBCH and now DogeChain.

Contract address: 0xa6a7a9c58f5dB6E9C1affb29A9FBD73390b46174

About NFTs

Wenlambo Nfts are 2500 of LAMBOS that are a part of the ecosystem around the token Lambo, these nfts will produce benefits for you with the staking and some more things that will come in the future. The mint of these nfts will start in august(day is not confirmed) and 30% of the mint will go to lambo liquidity to strength the lambo token.

About staking

Staking is the first utility that comes with lambo nfts, it gives profit to lambo holders that deposit their nfts in the system.
